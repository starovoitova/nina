Ultra Music Festival

March 20, 21, 22 — 2020 Bayfront Park Downtown Miami, FL

About

Ultra Music Festival is the 2020 annual electronic dance music festival held in March in Miami, Florida, USA. It is one of the largest festivals in the world. You will not have to be bored in your free time from UMF either. Miami Music Week and Winter Music Conference are also held at the same time as the festival. Miami's guests and residents will enjoy interesting conferences, the award ceremony of the leading international IDMA (International Dance Music Awards), presentations, useful workshops and much more.

The festival was founded in 1999 by Russell Fabish and Alex Oms. The festival is named after the 1997 Depeche Mode album Ultra.

Ultra is held in Miami at Bayfront Park. From 1999-2006 the festival was one-day, from 2007-2010 two-day, from 2011-2016 it was held in 3 days. In 2013, for the first time in the history of the festival, the UMF was held on weekends. In 2014, the festival returned to the format of one weekend.

Other Ultra Festivals are held in Buenos Aires (Argentina), Ibiza (Spain), Sao Paulo (Brazil), Santiago (Chile), Seoul (South Korea), Cape Town and Johannesburg (South Africa), Split and Hvar (Croatia), Bangkok (Thailand) and Tokyo (Japan). In December 2013, Ultra Worldwide announced the first release of the festival in Bogota, Colombia, which was held on February 20 and 21, 2014.

Artists

The 2020 lineup will be announced closer to the festival. The last edition included sets from Swedish House Mafia, David Guetta, DJ Snake, Carl Cox, Afrojack, Above & Beyond, Armin van Buuren, Adam Beyer, The Chainsmokers and Alesso.
